# Product Strategy: FocusTimer v1

**Based on:** `00-vision.md` (score: 3.6)
**Horizon:** next 2 quarters

---

## The bet

We're focusing on the **intention-setting ritual** as the thing that makes FocusTimer different from other timers. We'll prioritize the intention flow and the post-session reflection over adding more timer options.

We're not going to build team features right now — we want to nail the single-user experience first.

## Segment & sequencing

**First segment:** solo founders and indie makers who use focus tools but don't stick with them.

We'll start on iOS, then add Android later. First we'll ship the intention flow, then improve the reflection and streak features.

## The insight

People drop focus sessions when they lose motivation partway through. If we remind them of their intention, they're more likely to finish. Our early tests showed completion rates went up when we prompted users for an intention.

## Trade-offs

Focusing on solo users means we won't serve teams for a while, which could cost us some larger customers. We think it's worth it to get the core experience right.
