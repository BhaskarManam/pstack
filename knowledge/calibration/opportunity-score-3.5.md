## The question

Should we add streaks to encourage users to keep completing intentional sessions?

## Risks

- **Value:** Streaks work well in other apps, so they should help retention here too.
- **Usability:** Should be straightforward — streaks are a familiar pattern.
- **Feasibility:** Easy; it's just a counter on existing data.

## Riskiest assumption

The biggest risk is that streaks might make people game the system to keep their streak going. We should test whether streaks actually increase real engagement.

**Test:** Run streaks with a group of users and see if engagement goes up compared to users without streaks.
